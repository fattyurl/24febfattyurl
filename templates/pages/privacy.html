Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

What We Collect

When someone clicks a shortened link, we collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Referring URL
  • Country and city (from CDN headers, not IP geolocation databases)
  • A SHA-256 hash of the IP address (we never store raw IP addresses)

What We Don't Do

  • We never store raw IP addresses
  • We never use tracking pixels
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • We never show ads or interstitial pages
  • We never share individual click data with third parties

Account Data

If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication purposes. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Questions about our privacy practices? Contact us.
